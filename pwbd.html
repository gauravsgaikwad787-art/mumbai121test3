Why Work with a Small Company or Startup?

Small companies and startups can offer unique advantages for PwBD professionals:

  • Inclusive culture – Many are flexible and open to creating accommodating roles
  • Individual attention – Easier to build direct relationships with team leaders
  • Faster growth – Skills and efforts are quickly recognized
  • Flexibility – Opportunity to shape your role based on your strengths
  • Broader learning – Chance to explore multiple skills and functions

Core Jobs

We have carefully grouped major job roles into six Core Job categories based on high industry demand. These roles are consistently in demand across companies, increasing your chances of quick recruitment. Core Jobs are long-term career options—many professionals continue in these roles right up to retirement.

When you select a Core Job, your work profile will typically include one or more of the roles listed under that category.

Core Jobs 1 – IT Engineer

Kickstart your journey in tech! Perfect for those with computer science, IT, or coding skills.

Roles: Software Developer, Web Developer, QA/Software Tester, IT Support, Network Admin, Technical Support Executive

Core Jobs 2 – Non-IT Engineer

Engineers – this is your space! Join growing firms in core sectors.

Roles: Production Engineer, Quality Control/Assurance Engineer, Design Engineer (AutoCAD/SolidWorks), Maintenance Engineer, Site Engineer, Service Engineer

Core Jobs 3 – Sales & Marketing

Have a flair for communication and persuasion? These roles offer rapid growth and visibility.

Roles: Digital Marketing Executive, Sales Executive, Business Development Associate, Customer Acquisition Associate, Telecalling/Cold Calling Executive, SEO/SEM Assistant, Social Media Executive

Core Jobs 4 – General Roles

Support the heart of business operations. Ideal for graduates from any stream.

Roles: Data Entry, Admin Executive, General Management Trainee, Executive Assistant, Founder's Office Intern/Executive, Operations Assistant, Front Office Executive, Customer Support Executive, Customer Service Representative

Core Jobs 5 – Accounting, Finance & Purchase

Step into the world of business numbers and financial processes.

Roles: Finance Associates, Accounting Interns, Accounts Payable Specialists, Financial Analysts, Purchase Officer, Tender Executive

Core Jobs 6 – Legal Roles

Law graduates and compliance enthusiasts – find your space in legal-first support roles.

Roles: Legal cum Admin Executive, Junior Legal Officer, Legal & Compliance Executive, Documentation & Legal Support Executive, Tender Executive
