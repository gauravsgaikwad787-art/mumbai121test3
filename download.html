Download the Mumbai121 App

Get faster access to jobs, stay updated, and register on the go!

For Android Users

Installing Mumbai121.com on Android is quick and safe.

Alternative: Install Manually

  1. Open Chrome on your Android phone
  2. Visit https://www.mumbai121.com/
  3. Tap the three dots menu (⋮) in the top right
  4. Select "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  5. Tap Add, and the app icon will appear on your Home Screen

For iPhone Users

iPhone users can install Mumbai121.com using Safari.

Steps to Install:

  1. Open Safari browser on your iPhone
  2. Visit https://www.mumbai121.com/
  3. Tap the Share icon at the bottom
  4. Scroll down and choose "Add to Home Screen"
  5. Tap Add, and the app will appear on your Home Screen

Note: This feature only works in Safari browser. It won't work in Chrome or other browsers on iPhone.

Safe & Secure Installation

Your privacy and data security are our top priority.

  • The app is designed to offer a fast, secure, and lightweight experience
  • We do not ask for any app permissions, making this app extremely safe to use
  • If at any time the app unexpectedly requests permissions, do not grant them and report it to us immediately
  • All major browsers have built-in security features—any threat will be automatically detected and blocked by your browser
