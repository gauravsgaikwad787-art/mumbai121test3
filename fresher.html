Why Start with a Small Company or Startup?

Joining a small company or startup can give your career a powerful edge:

  • Faster growth – Get noticed and promoted quickly
  • Wider exposure – Learn multiple skills beyond your job title
  • Direct mentorship – Work closely with founders and decision-makers
  • More responsibility – Handle meaningful tasks from day one
  • Flexible environment – Work culture is often dynamic and open

Core Jobs

We have carefully grouped major job roles into six Core Job categories based on high industry demand. These roles are consistently in demand across companies, increasing your chances of quick recruitment.

When you select a Core Job, your work profile will typically include one or more of the roles listed under that category.

Core Jobs 1 – IT Engineer

Kickstart your journey in tech! Perfect for those with computer science, IT, or coding skills.

Roles: Software Developer, Web Developer, QA/Software Tester, IT Support, Network Admin, Technical Support Executive

Core Jobs 2 – Non-IT Engineer

Engineers – this is your space! Join growing firms in core sectors.

Roles: Production Engineer, Quality Control/Assurance Engineer, Design Engineer, Maintenance Engineer, Site Engineer, Service Engineer

Core Jobs 3 – Sales & Marketing

Have a flair for communication? These roles offer rapid growth and visibility.

Roles: Digital Marketing Executive, Sales Executive, Business Development Associate, SEO/SEM Assistant, Social Media Executive

Core Jobs 4 – General Roles

Support the heart of business operations. Ideal for graduates from any stream.

Roles: Data Entry, Admin Executive, Management Trainee, Executive Assistant, Operations Assistant, Customer Support Executive

Core Jobs 5 – Accounting, Finance & Purchase

Step into the world of business numbers.

Roles: Finance Associates, Accounting Interns, Accounts Payable Specialists, Financial Analysts, Purchase Officer

Core Jobs 6 – Legal Roles

Law graduates – find your space in legal-first support roles.

Roles: Legal cum Admin Executive, Junior Legal Officer, Legal & Compliance Executive, Documentation Executive
