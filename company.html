Who Can You Hire?

We help you recruit for entry-level roles from a pool of fresh graduates and PwBD candidates (PwBD candidates may be either freshers or experienced) – across six high-demand Core job categories.

Core Job Categories You Can Hire For:

Core Jobs 1 – IT Engineer

Perfect for tech-driven companies and startups.

Roles include: Software Developer, Web Developer, QA/Software Tester, IT Support, Network Admin, Technical Support Executive

Core Jobs 2 – Non-IT Engineer

For companies in manufacturing, production, or infrastructure.

Roles include: Production Engineer, Quality Control/Assurance Engineer, Design Engineer (AutoCAD/SolidWorks), Maintenance Engineer, Site Engineer, Service Engineer

Core Jobs 3 – Sales & Marketing

Boost your business with young talent in outreach and branding.

Roles include: Digital Marketing Executive, Sales Executive, Business Development Associate, Customer Acquisition Associate, Telecalling/Cold Calling Executive, SEO/SEM Assistant, Social Media Executive

Core Jobs 4 – General Roles

Ideal for everyday operations and support functions.

Roles include: Data Entry, Admin Executive, General Management Trainee, Executive Assistant, Founder's Office Intern/Executive, Operations Assistant, Front Office Executive, Customer Support Executive, Customer Service Representative

Core Jobs 5 – Accounting, Finance & Purchase

Let young professionals help you stay on top of your books, budgets, and buys – essential support for growing businesses.

Roles include: Finance Associates, Accounting Interns, Accounts Payable Specialists, Financial Analysts, Purchase Officer, Tender Executive

Core Jobs 6 – Legal Roles

Support your business with fresh legal talent to handle contracts, compliance, documentation, and day-to-day legal tasks.

Roles include: Legal cum Admin Executive, Junior Legal Officer, Legal & Compliance Executive, Documentation & Legal Support Executive, Tender Executive
