Frequently Asked Questions – Mumbai121.com

What is Mumbai121.com?

Mumbai121.com is a free platform connecting freshers and persons with benchmark disabilities (PwBD) with small companies and startups offering paid job opportunities in Mumbai.

Who can register on the platform?

  • Fresh graduates looking for their first job
  • Persons with benchmark disabilities (PwBD)
  • Small businesses, startups, and employers with real job openings

Is it really free for candidates and companies?

Yes! It is completely free for both candidates and companies. If you receive any call, email, or message asking for payment, please do not respond and report it to us immediately.

I am a PwBD candidate. Will I get additional support?

Yes. We have dedicated mentors and volunteers who help PwBD candidates:

  • Get interview-ready
  • Match with suitable roles
  • Get company-ready
  • Provide contact person info

We will help you list your job and find suitable candidates.

Do you provide interview preparation?

Yes, for selected candidates and PwBD applicants, our mentors help with:

  • Soft skills
  • Interview tips
  • Mock interviews

I want to volunteer or support this cause. What can I do?

We welcome volunteers! You can help with:

  • Mentoring freshers or PwBD
  • Outreach to jobseekers or employers
  • Data entry or social media

Contact us via WhatsApp or the Volunteer Form.

How can I deregister?

You just have to write an email from your registered email ID with the subject saying "deregister" and we will do the needful.

I have got a job. How can I inform you to take me out of the program?

You just have to write an email from your registered email ID with the subject saying "I got the Job" and we will do the needful.

I am a company and I want more candidate details?

You just have to write an email from your registered email ID with the subject saying "Send More candidate details" and we will do the needful based on availability.
